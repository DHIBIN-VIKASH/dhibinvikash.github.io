Professional Bio

I am an MBBS Intern (CRMI) at Government Karur Medical College & Hospital and a B.S. Data Science graduate from IIT Madras. I work at the intersection of medicine, technology, and research innovation, with a vision to integrate artificial intelligence and data-driven insights into clinical decision-making.

Through coursework and hands-on projects in machine learning, deep learning, large language models, and full-stack application development, I have built the technical foundation to bridge the gap between medical expertise and computational intelligence.

I am currently engaged in research focusing on systematic reviews and meta-analyses of randomized controlled trials (RCTs). My long-term goal is to develop clinically interpretable AI systems that enhance research quality, evidence synthesis, and patient outcomes.
