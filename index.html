Professional Bio

Dhibin Vikash is an MBBS Intern(CRMI) at Government Karur Medical College & Hospital and a B.S. Data Science graduate from IIT Madras. He works at the intersection of medicine, technology, and research innovation, with a vision to integrate artificial intelligence and data-driven insights into clinical decision-making.

Through coursework and hands-on projects in machine learning, deep learning, large language models, and full-stack application development, he has built the technical foundation to bridge the gap between medical expertise and computational intelligence.

He is currently engaged in research focusing on systematic reviews and meta-analyses of randomized controlled trials (RCTs). His long-term goal is to develop clinically interpretable AI systems that enhance research quality, evidence synthesis, and patient outcomes.
